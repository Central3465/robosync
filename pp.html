RoboSync Privacy Policy

Welcome to the Privacy Policy of RoboSync. Before you proceed, please read the following information carefully.

1. Information We Collect

RoboSync will never ask for your personal information. All data is anonymized and used only to provide the service. We will only collect your Roblox and Discord username.

2. How We Use Your Information

RoboSync will only use your information to provide precise information on our platforms.

3. Cookies

To maintain a pleasurable RoboSync experience, we ask that you enable all cookies.

4. Security

RoboSync's Security Measures are set to a extreme level. We protect the privacy of each user by using secure protocols and encryption methods.

We take the security of your data very seriously and ensure it is encrypted when in transit or at rest.

5. Third-Party Links

RoboSync will never redirect you to an unsecure website. The only website RoboSync will redirect you is roblox.com and discord.com.

6. Changes to Privacy Policy

Outline the procedure for updating or modifying the Privacy Policy and notify users to check periodically for changes.

If you have any questions or concerns about our Privacy Policy, please contact us at support@robosync.net.
